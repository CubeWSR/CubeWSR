Best Actors:

#1: Lee Jong Suk

Who?

Lee Jong Suk is a South Korean actor and model who started his career young, making his debut in 2005 as a runway model. He actually holds the title of the youngest male model ever to participate in Seoul Fashion Week! While he began in modeling, Lee Jong Suk transitioned into acting and achieved his breakthrough role in the drama "School 2013." Since then, he's become well-known for his starring roles in many popular dramas like "I Can Hear Your Voice," "Pinocchio," "W," and "While You Were Sleeping." He's known for his versatility, strong acting skills, and ability to connect with audiences.

Why?

Lee Jong Suk's rise to fame is a combination of his impressive acting skills and undeniable star power. He's not afraid to challenge himself with diverse roles, showcasing his versatility. Whether it's the comedic timing required for a rom-com like "Prosecutor Princess" or the emotional depth needed for dramas like "While You Were Sleeping" where he plays a character who can hear people's thoughts, Lee Jong Suk brings his characters to life in a believable way. This wide range keeps audiences engaged and excited to see what he'll do next. But Lee Jong Suk goes beyond just playing the part. He's a skilled actor who delivers nuanced performances. He can express subtle emotions with a flick of an eyebrow or a slight change in tone, and he can also convincingly portray characters going through intense situations, making viewers feel deeply invested in his journey on screen. His talent is undeniable, and it's not just fans who recognize it. Lee Jong Suk has won awards like "Best New Actor" for his role in "School 2013," and he's been a leading actor in numerous hit dramas like "Pinocchio" and "W." These achievements not only showcase his talent but also demonstrate his ability to connect with audiences on a large scale, solidifying his place as a top Korean actor. Lee Jong Suk's dedication to his craft and his natural charisma have also earned him a massive fanbase. He portrays relatable characters with a charm that captivates viewers, making him a household name in South Korea and gaining him international recognition as well.

#2: Ahn Hyo-seop


Who?

Ahn Hyo-seop, a Canadian-born South Korean actor, has taken the Korean drama scene by storm. After returning to Korea at 17, he pursued acting, even turning down a spot in K-pop group GOT7. His breakout role came in "Still 17" (2018), and he's since starred in hits like "Dr. Romantic 2" and "Business Proposal." His versatility and talent make him a rising star in Korean entertainment.

Why?

Ahn Hyo-seop has become a rising star in Korean dramas, capturing the hearts of audiences both domestically and internationally. Born in Canada, he returned to South Korea at 17 and embarked on his acting journey, even famously choosing acting over a potential spot in the K-pop group GOT7. His breakout role in "Still 17" (2018) showcased his talent, and he hasn't looked back since, starring in hit dramas like "Dr. Romantic 2" and the wildly popular "Business Proposal." A key reason for his fame is his stellar acting and impressive versatility. He can seamlessly transition from portraying the passionate innocence of a high school student in "Still 17" to the stoic professionalism of a doctor in "Dr. Romantic 2," all with incredible depth and believability. This range keeps viewers engaged and eager to see what roles he tackles next. Furthermore, his talent has been recognized by the industry. Awards like "Best New Actor" for "Still 17" and acknowledgements for his work in other dramas solidify his reputation as a highly skilled and rising star. Ahn Hyo-seop's fame isn't just about awards though. He's been fortunate to star in several highly-rated dramas, particularly "Business Proposal," which gained international acclaim through streaming platforms. This exposure has significantly expanded his fanbase, making him a household name not just in Korea but across the globe. Finally, there's an undeniable charisma that Ahn Hyo-seop brings to the screen. He possesses the ability to deliver comedic timing, heart-wrenching emotional moments, and everything in between, making him captivating to watch. This combination of talent, charisma, and involvement in successful dramas has propelled Ahn Hyo-seop to fame, solidifying his position as a talented and versatile actor with an exciting future in Korean entertainment.

#3: Kim Soo-hyun


Who?

Kim Soo-hyun, a powerhouse in Korean entertainment, has skyrocketed to become one of the highest-paid actors and a fan favorite. His meteoric rise started in 2011 with the teen drama "Dream High," showcasing his comedic talent and versatility. He fearlessly tackles diverse projects, from historical dramas to fantasy romances and thrillers, with believable performances that have earned him numerous awards and solidified him as a top actor. His influence extends beyond Korea, with his dramas propelling him to Hallyu stardom, making him a household name across Asia and a global icon. Since his return from military service, he carefully selects roles that challenge him, showcasing his dedication to his craft and ensuring his continued reign as a top Korean actor.

Why?

Kim Soo-hyun is a South Korean acting powerhouse who has skyrocketed to international fame. His career exploded in 2011 with the teen drama "Dream High." Unlike your typical teen idol role, he showcased both comedic talent and surprising emotional depth. This versatility launched him into the spotlight and established him as a rising star. Kim Soo-hyun doesn't shy away from challenges. He actively seeks out diverse projects, from historical epics like "Moon Embracing the Sun" to fantastical romances like "You Who Came from the Star." His dedication to each character and his ability to deliver believable performances have earned him critical acclaim and numerous awards. The phenomenal success of dramas like "You Who Came from the Star" propelled him to Hallyu stardom, making him a household name across Asia and a global icon. Even after completing his military service, Kim Soo-hyun remains dedicated to his craft, choosing roles that challenge him and showcase his ever-evolving skills. This dedication ensures his continued reign as a top Korean actor.

Top Actress:

#1: Im Yoon-ah


Who?

Im Yoon-ah, or Yoona as she's known by fans, is a multi-talented South Korean star who shines in both music and acting. She rose to fame as a captivating vocalist and visual center of the legendary girl group Girls' Generation (SNSD), a frontrunner of the Korean Wave (Hallyu). But Yoona isn't afraid to branch out. In 2019, she launched a successful solo music career with her debut EP. As an actress, Yoona displays impressive range, captivating audiences in dramas like "Love Rain" and "The K2" and films like the "Confidential Assignment" series. Her influence extends beyond entertainment; she's a highly sought-after brand ambassador, solidifying her status as a Korean icon with a global fanbase.

Why?

Yoona, a name synonymous with Korean entertainment, is a multi-talented star who shines brightly. Her rise to fame began with Girls' Generation (SNSD), a powerhouse girl group that dominated the Korean music scene and became a leader of the Korean Wave. Yoona's captivating voice and stage presence were a key part of their success. But Yoona isn't afraid of a challenge. She ventured into solo music in 2019, showcasing her artistic independence with her debut EP. Her talents extend beyond music though. Yoona is a skilled actress who has captivated audiences with her range, from romantic dramas to action-packed thrillers. Her award-winning performances have solidified her reputation as a versatile talent. Yoona's influence isn't limited to music and acting. She's a highly sought-after brand ambassador, a testament to her ability to connect with audiences and her undeniable beauty. This widespread influence has solidified her as a true Korean icon and a star with a global fanbase.

#2: Kim Sejeong


Who?

Kim Sejeong, or Sejeong to fans, is a rising Korean star captivating audiences with her singing and acting talents. She rose to fame on the show "Produce 101," landing a spot in the project group I.O.I. Even before their disbandment, Sejeong showcased her ambition with a solo debut and a music show win, a record for fastest soloists. She continues to release music, exploring themes of self-discovery and resonating with fans. Sejeong's not just a singer though. She's a blossoming actress who impressed in her debut lead role in "School 2017." Since then, she's starred in diverse shows like "The Uncanny Counter" and "Business Proposal," solidifying her versatility. Award recognition for both singing and acting highlights her potential. With her vocals, acting skills, and charisma, Sejeong is a force to be reckoned with in Korean entertainment. She's here to stay.

Why?

Kim Sejeong gained fame primarily through her participation in the South Korean survival reality show "Produce 101" in 2016, where her talent, charm, and vocals garnered widespread recognition, leading her to finish in second place overall. This exposure propelled her into the project girl group I.O.I, further boosting her popularity and establishing her as a notable figure in the K-pop industry. Known for her powerful vocals and versatility, Sejeong has continued to showcase her singing abilities through various performances and solo releases. In addition to her musical talents, she has also made a name for herself in acting, appearing in dramas such as "School 2017," "I Wanna Hear Your Song," and "The Uncanny Counter," demonstrating her range as an entertainer. Beyond her artistic endeavors, Sejeong has become a sought-after celebrity for endorsements and brand partnerships, solidifying her status as a prominent and multifaceted entertainer in South Korea.

#3: Yeom Hye-ran


Who?

South Korean actress Yeom Hye-ran has carved a successful path across film, television, and theater stages. Born in 1976, she honed her skills in theater productions before transitioning to the screen in 2003. While she's known for her captivating supporting roles in a diverse range of projects like "Memories of Murder" and "Guardian: The Lonely and Great God," she also delivered a critically acclaimed lead performance as a single mother in the film "Life," winning her the Best Actress award at the Jeonju International Film Festival. Most recently, her portrayal in the revenge drama "The Glory" earned her a nomination for Best Supporting Actress at the Baeksang Arts Awards.

Why?

South Korean actress Yeom Hye-ran has risen to prominence thanks to a remarkable combination of talents. Her versatility allows her to shine in comedic and dramatic roles alike, breathing life into each character with depth and nuance. This consistent ability to deliver powerful performances has earned her the respect and admiration of both audiences and directors. Furthermore, Yeom Hye-ran boasts a long and impressive career. Starting in theater productions in 1999 and transitioning to screen acting in 2003, she has become a prolific force in the Korean entertainment industry. Her frequent appearances in popular dramas and films have made her a familiar and beloved face, consistently gracing our screens with her talent. While Yeom Hye-ran is widely recognized for her captivating supporting roles, she has also proven she can carry a film on her own. Her critically acclaimed lead performance in "Life" earned her the prestigious Best Actress award at the Jeonju International Film Festival, solidifying her reputation as a highly skilled and impactful performer. Adding to her acclaim, Yeom Hye-ran's recent nomination for Best Supporting Actress in "The Glory" further reinforces her talent and keeps her in the public eye. Awards like this serve as a testament to her significant contributions to Korean cinema and television. In a nutshell, Yeom Hye-ran's dedication to her craft, her impressive range, and her consistent ability to deliver unforgettable performances across various genres have cemented her fame as a treasured actress in the Korean entertainment landscape.
